Absolute XPath: /html/body/..././button

Text-based XPath: //button[text()='Find by Text']
Attribute XPath: //input[@name='email']
Email is required.
Starts-with: //button[starts-with(@id, 'btn')]
Contains: //button[contains(@class, 'btn')]
XPath with AND/OR: //button[@type='button' AND @class='btn btn-warning']
Parent Navigation: //button[@id='childBtn']/parent::div
Following Sibling: //label[text()='Name']/following-sibling::input
Position-based: //button[position()=1] or //button[1]
